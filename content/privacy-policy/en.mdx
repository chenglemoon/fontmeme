# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 8, 2025

## 1. What We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. We also do not log IP addresses, browser details, or other technical data. You can use the service without logging in or sharing any personal data.

## 2. Text Input and Generated Content

Text you enter is processed only in your browser. We do not store, back up, or analyze your text input. All processing happens locally on your device.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to track visit counts and page usage to help us improve the service. The data is anonymized and does not include personal information or uploaded content.

## 4. Cookie Usage

This site does not use cookies. Your browser will not be written with any cookie data during use.

## 5. Data Control and Feedback

You have full control over your text input and generated content. If you have any questions about privacy or data handling, feel free to contact us using the information above.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hi@fontmeme.fun. We're here to help.
